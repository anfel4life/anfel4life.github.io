Boundless opportunities for your business

What is Big Data

Big data is growing technology area where innovative solutions bring new ways to extract and use value from information. It can be described as large volume of structured and unstructured data, that can be analyzed for insights that drive to making wise strategy and great business ideas.

More and more connected to internet things come over to our lives, that starts from fitness trackers and cellphones and ends with cars and heavy machines. These things bring a lot of data which can be analyzed, processed, transformed into insights and used or sold to consumers or companies.

Data-Driven Strategy

Analytics and visualization of big data can bring company at new level of data-driven, where facts (data) rather than opinions are used to pick crucial choice. A data-driven company is an organization where data is a part of the core business strategy and every person operates data to make better decisions and reduce uncertainty.

Being data-driven means making deal with massive quantities of unstructured data like text, video, voice. Before now this operation needed the whole IT department which had tough time extracting information from these data resources, but nowadays with help of new technology it requires lesser human resources and can be reached by data mining process.

Organisation Processes Optimisation

Data can help to understand weakness and pitfalls of your company’s organisation and development process. It can bring your company at new stage of organisation management and help to create organisation transparency by making data more easily accessible to relevant employees in a timely manner.

Boost of Customer Intelligence

Machine learning is delicate way of analytics that helps derive insights from data to figure out customer preferences, behaviour and habits that could be used to develop better customer loyalty. Awareness of who is your customer gives you an opportunity for better satisfaction of their needs, that leads to more happiness of your clients.

Better Customer Experience

Innovative manufacturing companies pack their products with sensors that are used by the company to understand how their product is being used, diagnose breakages, develop innovations and new features. Moreover data helps their clients to experience the product at new quality level. These companies are not only using data to make analytics and predictions, but they also sell it as a part of the product.

According to our investigation, working with Big Data can be challenging for personal without technical background. We believe that benefit of Big Data can be available for everyone.

Here in Cordbex we provide for our clients best data visualisation and analytics experience as a part of our solutions. Understandable even for non-technical people charts, graphs, tables can give valuable information for company's personal that is necessary to make crucial decisions.

Fast Calculation of Risk Portfolio

Using huge amount of data opens new opportunities for risk managements. Big Data can be used to simulate scenarios that help to determine risk concentration, or derive patterns that recognize potential fraud more precisely. With help of new technologies operation of risk portfolio recalculation takes minutes, so you have data to make key decision in a short time.
